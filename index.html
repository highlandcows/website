Nick Jacobs | Highland Cows

The first question I get when people come to my web site is, "Why Highland Cows?"

It's actually not that complicated. Highland Cows was a company that I started some years ago when I was doing some contract work for a friend. When that gig wound down, I still felt attached to the domain so I've kept it for my professional prescence on the web.

What I Do

I'm a coder with 30+ years experience in the technology industry, the bulk of that in financial services. I'm currently working for a payments for small businesses start-up in Cape Town, Yoco Technologies.

I also work with a local volunteer organization, Color Code, teaching and mentoring aspiring coders. You can learn more about this awesome organization at colorcode.org.za.

Finally, if you really want to find out more about what I've done, I have a LinkedIn profile.
